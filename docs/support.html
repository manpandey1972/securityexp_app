Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download GoAegent and sign up using your phone number (you'll receive an SMS with a one-time verification code) or use Sign in with Apple for a quick, privacy-friendly sign-up.

How do I make a call?

Find an expert from the home screen, open their profile, and tap the audio or video call button.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. All your data including messages, call history, and profile information will be permanently deleted.

Is my chat private?

Yes. All chat messages in GoAegent are end-to-end encrypted (E2EE). Only you and the person you're chatting with can read the messages — not GoAegent, not any server.

How do I book a session with an expert?

From the expert's profile, tap Book a Session. Choose an available time slot, confirm the details, and submit your request. You'll receive a confirmation and a reminder notification before the session starts.

How do I report or block a user?

  • Open the chat or user profile and tap the ⋮ menu (three dots).
  • Select Report to flag inappropriate behaviour, or Block to prevent further contact.
  • Blocked users can be managed under Profile → Blocked Users.

I'm not receiving call notifications

  • Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings for GoAegent
  • On iOS, ensure Allow Notifications is turned on
  • Check that you have a stable internet connection

The app is crashing or not working properly

  • Make sure you're running the latest version of GoAegent
  • Try closing and reopening the app
  • Restart your device
  • If the issue persists, contact us at goaegent.privacy@gmail.com with a description of the problem

For Experts

How do I set up my availability?

Go to Profile → Availability and set the days and times you are open for bookings. Clients can only book sessions during these windows.

How do I manage my expert profile?

Go to Profile → Edit Profile to update your bio, skills, languages, hourly rate, and profile picture. A complete profile improves your visibility in search results.

A client didn't show up for a session

Contact us at goaegent.privacy@gmail.com with the session details. We review no-show cases individually and will advise on next steps.
